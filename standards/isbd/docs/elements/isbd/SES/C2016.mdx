# Title And Statement Of Responsibility Area Encoding Scheme

## Element Reference
<ElementReference frontMatter={frontMatter} />

## Definition

<div className="guid">
This element within the <strong>Material Description Area (Area 5)</strong> provides a statement of 
<strong>additional physical characteristics</strong> of the resource beyond its extent and dimensions. 
This includes details like material composition, presence of colour or illustrations, and various technical specifications.
</div>

## Required Elements

<div className="guid">
<p>All elements are optional:</p>
<ul>
  <li><strong>Composition of material:</strong> Word or phrase indicating what the resource is made of 
    (e.g., "wood," "nitrate," "transparency").</li>
  <li><strong>Presence of illustrations:</strong> Abbreviation "ill." (or equivalent). 
    Can specify "all ill." or "chiefly maps".</li>
  <li><strong>Presence of colour:</strong> "col." for colour, "b&w" for black and white.</li>
  <li><strong>Reduction ratio (Microforms):</strong> Numerical ratio (e.g., "14x," "150x") 
    or descriptive terms (e.g., "low reduction").</li>
  <li><strong>Presence or absence of sound:</strong> "sd." for sound, "si." for silent, when not implicit in 
    the specific material designation (e.g., for film reels). Brief explanatory phrases can be added (e.g., "(Beta HiFi)").</li>
  <li><strong>Other technical specifications:</strong><ul>
      <li><strong>Frame alignment</strong> (for microforms/filmstrips, 
        e.g., "cine mode," "comic mode").</li>
      <li><strong>Process or method of reproduction</strong> (for still images, 
        e.g., "drypoint, aquatint").</li>
      <li><strong>Playing speed</strong> (e.g., "33 1/3 rpm," "19 cm/s").</li>
      <li><strong>Recording method</strong> (e.g., "stereo," "mono").</li>
      <li><strong>Groove direction/size</strong> (for sound discs).</li>
      <li><strong>Number of tape tracks</strong>.</li>
      <li><strong>Track configuration</strong> (for sound reels with 4+ tracks).</li>
      <li><strong>Number of sound channels</strong> (e.g., "mono," "stereo").</li>
      <li><strong>Equalization</strong>.</li>
      <li><strong>Noise reduction</strong>.</li>
    </ul>
  </li>
</ul>
</div>

## Punctuation Rules

<div className="guid">
This statement is preceded by <strong>:</strong> within Area 5.
</div>

## Relationship to ISBD Standards

<div className="guid">
This is element <strong>5.2</strong> within Area 5. 
It provides granular details about the physical manifestation of the resource.
</div>

## Relationship to Other SES

<div className="guid">
<ul>
  <li>It is a component of the "Material Description Area Encoding Scheme."</li>
  <li>If a resource is available in alternative formats with different physical details, this information may be 
    omitted here and given in <strong>Area 8 (Resource Identifier and Terms of Availability Area)</strong> 
    or the <strong>Note Area (Area 7)</strong>.</li>
  <li>Notes providing additional physical details or peculiar characteristics are given in 
    the <strong>Note Area (Area 7)</strong>.</li>
</ul>
</div>
